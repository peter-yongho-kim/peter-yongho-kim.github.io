Hi, I'm Peter Yongho Kim (김용호). 

My main interest is AI4Brain, or unraveling the secrets of our brain and applying them to various tasks using AI. 

This includes the following research topics:
- Building "foundation models" for brain images (mainly fMRI) and applying them to various tasks.
- Visual brain decoding, where vision information is directly decoded from brain images.
